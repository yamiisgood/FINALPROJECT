About this Blogspot

This blogspot is a journey through William's travel experiences and dreams. He has already explored the historic Fort Santiago, immersing himself in the rich history of Manila. Additionally, he enjoyed the serene and beautiful beaches of El Nido, indulging in swimming and the tranquil surroundings. However, his ultimate dream destination is Boracay. He looks forward to experiencing its stunning white sand beaches and vibrant atmosphere. This blogspot serves as a diary of his past adventures and future aspirations.
